### 🚀 What I'm Architecting Right Now

I focus on building systems that scale, whether it's optimizing transaction states or deploying local LLMs. Currently based in Bangalore and working on:

* 🤖 **[Krythos]** - Developing infrastructure for AI Sales Development Representative (SDR) agents, utilizing open-source models and local LLM runtimes (Ollama) for B2B automation.
* 💳 **[ReDeem]** - Engineering a P2P credit card reward voucher marketplace backed by a guaranteed escrow state machine.
* 📈 **Quant Backtesting Engine** - Building modular Python engines to test mutual fund and direct equity allocation strategies against historical market data.
* 🎥 **[@daily_commit]** - Documenting the 10-hour build sprints, gym regimens, and business case studies.
